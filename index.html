Internship FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship programs.

When do applications for internships open?
Our internship applications are always open! You can apply anytime that works best for you — we’re here whenever you’re ready to start..
Do we get stipend?
Yes, you’ll receive a stipend — but only after completing two full months of the internship. It’s our way of rewarding your commitment and consistency!
Are internships paid?
Our internships aren’t paid — instead, students pay a small fee to enroll. Think of it as an investment in hands-on experience, skill-building, and a certified boost to your career.
Will I get placement?
We offer placement opportunities after your internship period. If you perform well, you can join us as a full-time team member!.
Is both offline and online available?
Yes, we offer both offline and online internships to fit your schedule and preferences. You can choose the mode that works best for you!.
What kind of learning experience can I expect from your internships?
We provide hands-on, experiential training that lets you dive right into real-world projects. It’s all about learning by doing, so you gain skills that truly matter!
Do you offer any discounts if we apply as a group?
Absolutely! If your group has more than 10 members, each person only needs to pay ₹999. It’s a great way to learn together without burning a hole in your pocket!
Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?
Yes! Upon successful completion, you’ll get a certificate to showcase your skills and boost your resume.
